The app shall be developed natively for the Android platform using Kotlin.
The app shall target Android API level 26 (Android 8.0 Oreo) as the minimum SDK.
The app shall provide secure user authentication via username and password.
The app shall support biometric authentication (fingerprint/face) for login.
The app shall encrypt all sensitive user data both at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols.
The app shall display real-time account balances for all linked accounts.
The app shall display detailed transaction history with filtering capabilities.
The app shall enable secure funds transfers between user-linked accounts.
The app shall enable secure funds transfers to external beneficiaries.
The app shall provide functionality for bill payments to pre-registered payees.
The app shall integrate with existing banking backend RESTful APIs.
The app shall utilize Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for push notifications.
The app shall adhere to Google Material Design guidelines for user interface and experience.
The app shall be responsive and functional across common Android screen sizes and orientations.
The app shall maintain an average loading time under 3 seconds on a stable internet connection.
The app shall be packaged as an Android App Bundle (AAB) for Google Play Store submission.
The app shall comply with all Google Play Store developer program policies.
The app shall implement session management with automatic logout after inactivity.
The app shall provide an in-app help/FAQ section and customer support contact options.
The app shall provide access to view and download monthly account statements.
The app shall include analytics for crash reporting and anonymous usage statistics.
The app shall undergo security penetration testing prior to release.
The app shall provide multi-factor authentication for sensitive transactions.
